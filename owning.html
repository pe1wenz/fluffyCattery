Information About Acquiring A Maine Coon Kitten

  • Flyffy Fluffy Maine Coon kittens may go to their new homes after they are 4 mohths old.
  • They will be examined by veterinarians.
  • All Kittens will be vaccined and spayed/neutered.
  • All Kittens will have a micro-chip that has owner's information.

    • For The Purchasers:

  • Cat(s) must be in-door only.
  • The Cat(s) MUST NOT be declawed.
  • The Cat(s) must be feeded by high quality food
  • Any kitten in the "Kitten" page that does not showing "reserved" means the kitten is available for reservation

    • Pricing:

  • Our Maine coon kittens are priced at $1500(US).
  • Each kitten needs a non-refundable deposite of $500(US)

    • Visiting the Cattery:

  • We are happy to welcome everyone who wants to see our lovely cats!
  • All visitors needs to send us an email for us to arrange time.

    • Delivery Options:

    Self-pickup

    Self-pickup from the cattery is free and it is the best option for your kitten!

    We can deliver the kitten for you!*

    We can deliver the kitten directly to your home by driving. Driving is less stressful compair to flying for the kitten.
    We will charge $40(US)/hour for the roundtrip.
    For example: If we need to drive 2.5 hours for one way, we will charge 5 X $40(US) which is $200(US).

    We can meet part way!*

    We can arrange time and location for meeting at the part way!
    We will charge $40(US)/hour for the round trip(destination ==> halfway location) for this option.


    * The buyer needs to pay the full amount for the kitten and the delivery fee before the delivery time.
