Owning a Maine coon cat

How to own one of our cats?

Owning a kitten is an exciting thing! There are some rules we would like you to know before start applying for one of our lovely kitten.

  • A $500 non-refundable,non-transferable deposite for every kitten.
  • Every kitten needs to be at least 4 months old before sending to its new home
  • Any kitten in the "Kitten" page that does not showing "reserved" means the kitten is available for reservation

What is the cost of one of your kitten?

Our Maine coon kittens are priced at $2000. Every kitten will come with following items:

  • proof of spaying or neutering
  • proof of vaccines
  • proof of microchip
  • one pet carrier
  • dry cat food
