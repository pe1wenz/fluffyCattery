Information About Acquiring A Maine Coon Kitten

  • Fluffy Fluffy Maine Coon kittens may go to their new homes after they are 4 months old.
  • They will be examined by veterinarians.
  • All Kittens will be vaccinated and spayed/neutered.
  • All Kittens will have a micro-chip that has owner's information.

    • For The Purchasers:

  • Cat(s) must be in door only.
  • The Cat(s) MUST NOT be declawed.
  • The Cat(s) must be fed high quality food
  • Any kitten in the "Kitten" page that does not show "reserved" means the kitten is available for reservation

    • Pricing:

  • Our Maine coon kittens are priced at $1500(US).
  • Each kitten needs a non-refundable deposit of $500(US)
  • If buyer wants CFA's registration slip for the kitten, the application for the registration slip is $60(US).
  • If buyer wants CFA's pedigree, the application for the pedigree is $100(US).
  • We accept Zelle, Venmo, Paypal, Check, Cash and Wire for any payments.

    • Visiting the Cattery:

  • We are happy to welcome everyone who wants to see our lovely cats!
  • All visitors needs to send us an email or a phone call for us to arrange time.

    • Delivery Options:

    Self-pickup

    Self-pickup from the cattery is free and it is the best option for your kitten!

    We can deliver the kitten for you!*

    We can deliver the kitten directly to your home by driving. Driving is less stressful compair to flying for the kitten.
    Price for delivery needs to be discussed by email or phone
    * The buyer needs to pay the full amount for the kitten and the delivery fee before the delivery time.
