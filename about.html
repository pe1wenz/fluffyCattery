We are Fluffy Cattery

We are Fluffy Cattery located in Virginia. Cats are a big part of our family, we love their passion and energy. Every cat and kitten is raised by our hands. After several months of time living together, saying goodbye to “our childrens” and giving it to another person is a challenge for us. We wish “our childrens” can live happily in their new family.

We raise a small number of litters every year. Our belief is that cats are “our childrens” and they need the best possible care from us. All the kittens are raised with love. Our kittens are trusting humans as their own parents.
