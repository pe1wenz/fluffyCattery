We are Fluffy Fluffy

We are Fluffy Fluffy located in Christiansburg, Virginia.

We are a CFA-certified cattery. All of our breeding cats are registered.

What is Cat Fanciers' Association(CFA)?
Our CFA-Page website:
https://cfa.org/find-a-breeder/listing/fluffyfluffy/

Cats are a big part of our family, we love their passion and energy. Every cat and kitten is raised by our hands. After several months of time living together, saying goodbye to “our children” and giving them to another person is a challenge for us. We wish “our children” can live happily in their new family.

We raise a small number of litters every two years. Our belief is that cats are “our children” and they need the best possible care from us. All the kittens are raised with love. Our kittens are trusting humans as their own parents.
