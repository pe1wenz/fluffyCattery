Be careful with Scammers!

Recently we received multiple calls from people asking for kittens they found on scammers’ websites. These scammers have a very similar name to ours, and people have mistaken us for the same breeder as those scammers. We would like to request everyone who is looking to buying a pure bred Maine Coon to read this post and avoid possible scammers on the internet.

Here are two things that only legitimate breeders will have:

1: The breeder MUST be registered on TICA/CFA, and able to provide kitten’s registration from TICA/CFA.
Link for TICA: http://tica.org/maine-coon-breeders
Link for CFA: https://find-a-breeder.cfa.org/

2. The breeder will always agree to make a live video call to show you that the kittens are real.

If the breeder you find does not meet any of the two requirements mentioned above, it is not a legitimate breeder.
Never send out your deposit before verifying the kitten is real. You can ask for video call or visit to the breeder’s place.
We wish everyone have a wonderful day and feel free to share this post with others.
